Mission !

We don’t just give students an education and experiences that set them up for success in a career. We help them succeed in their career—to discover a field they’re passionate about and dare to lead it.

Message From Principle

Welcome to MGBS, a learning organization that thrives always to learn the best practices in local and global context and keep transforming itself as a whole institution! MGBS is the institution that believes in providing opportunities and environment for children to aspire for high achievement with practice of values. MGBS Team is well aware of the fact that every individual child has innate potential that has to be identified and nurtured in time. Therefore, it is well prepared to encourage every student’s insightful learning with confidence and skills.

Objectives

1.To provide quality education at reasonable fee structure
2.To encourage the students to identify their inner potentialities
3.To prepare the students to meet the challenges of related fields rationally
4.To enhance practical knowledge and skill of the students
5. To build up students confidence in facing all kinds of national as well as international exams of their levels
6. To facilitate pleasant and suitable learning environment
7. To make the students familiar with the modern technology to cope up with the globalized.
8. To produce and provide medium level human resources

Salient Features

1. Morning to Day Shift Classes
2. Nepali and English Medium of teaching
3. Well-equipped science laboratories and Library
4. Managed and run by highly qualified and experienced and trained teachers
5. Emphasis on games and sports cultural activities, educational tours and exposures
6.Psychological and career counseling for the students
7. Access to graduate on technical Education Special computer classes with Well-equipped Computer Labs
8. Audio-visuals classes with multimedia projectors
9. Peaceful and healthy Learning environment
10. Regular evaluation system of internal exams and feedback sharing
11. Reward for intelligent, disciplined and punctual
12.Scholarship scheme for intelligent and deprived students
13.Opportunity to join any faculty and subjects according to the student’s choice and their ability.
